Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We want you to be confident in working with Codalithic. This policy outlines how refunds are handled across our services.

Deposits

Project deposits are non-refundable once work has begun, as they cover initial planning, design, and resource allocation.

Milestone-Based Projects

For projects billed in milestones, refunds may be issued for any milestone not yet started. Completed milestones are non-refundable.

Subscription / Retainer Services

Monthly automation or maintenance retainers can be cancelled anytime; the current billing cycle already paid for is non-refundable, but no further charges will occur after cancellation.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Third-party costs already incurred (domains, hosting, app store fees, premium plugins/themes, dialer/AI platform setup fees).
  • Completed and approved deliverables.

How to Request a Refund

Email info@codalithic.com with your project details and reason for the request. We'll review and respond within 5 business days.
