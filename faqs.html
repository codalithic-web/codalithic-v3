FAQs

Questions, answered.

What services does Codalithic offer?+

We cover AI automation (chat bots, call agents, AIO), Android & iOS app development, custom-coded websites, e-commerce, WordPress, and Shopify builds — all from one team.

How long does a typical website take to build?+

A custom-coded marketing site usually takes 2–4 weeks; e-commerce and app builds typically run 4–8 weeks depending on scope.

Can you host my site, or do I need my own hosting?+

We can deploy on platforms like Vercel, or hand the project off so you can host it yourself on a provider such as Hostinger.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?+

Yes — every project includes a support window, and we offer ongoing maintenance plans for updates, monitoring, and improvements.

What is AIO (Artificial Intelligence Optimization)?+

AIO is the practice of structuring your content and data so AI assistants and AI-powered search surfaces correctly represent and recommend your business.

How does the AI call agent consent process work?+

Before we set up AI call automation, we collect explicit consent from you to verify and configure your number through our dialing partner, in line with applicable telecom regulations.

Do you build for WordPress as well as custom code?+

Yes — we offer both. Custom code gives more flexibility and performance; WordPress is ideal if you want to edit content yourself afterward.
